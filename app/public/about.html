About Me



Megan Vogler

Hi, I'm Megan, my daughter's name is Tamaryn. She is 4 years old and has been diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder since she was about 2. "Hyper" is not strong enough of a descriptor for this tornado-like child. Taking her places can be extremely difficult, with her impatience for lines, headbutting tantrums and inability to follow my simple instruction of "STOP!" as she energetically runs towards oncoming traffic, taking her places involves a lot of forethought and patience.

The goal of this blog is to encourage myself to take her places and hopefully help a few people learn from my mistakes along the way. I will post about places to visit in the greater Nashville area and resources I have used (or have heard about) in the local Mount Juliet and larger Nashville areas.
